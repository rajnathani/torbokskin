Welcome here! Torbok is a book trade network for students studying at universities in the Greater Toronto Area. If you have a book to sell then post your book offering by clicking the 'Post Book' button below. If you've come to buy books then use the search box to search for the book you want or use the to instantly get notified when a book is posted matching a set of keywords which you define.

    Notify Me

    Notify Me is a simple service to help you get instantly notified when the books you want to buy are posted by someone on torbok.

    All you do is provide a set of keywords like 'ECO227', 'Calculus', 'Java'. The millisecond someone posts a book which matches your spider web of keywords, the server will shoot you an email informing you about it!
